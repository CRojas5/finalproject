Cooper's Fact

These are the most important facts about me. What I love, hate, my favorite treat, and of course when my birthday is. That's a fact you must know if I'm to like you, but let's be honest I never really like anyone (until they give me a treat). But if you know these facts about me maybe I'll like you before you give me a treat. Keyword being maybe.

Love

The thing I love more for a fact is eating and sleeping. Especially sleeping, my favorite place to sleep is in the middle of the bed so my owner can't fall asleep comfortably.

Hate

The thing I hate the most for a fact is the vacum. It's out to get me, once it turns on I run away. I'm scared for my owners life, I hope it doesn't get her one day. But I warned her.

Birthday

Fun fact my birthday is October 31st on Halloween. On this day I tend to dress up like a hot dog and bark at all the little kids that dare knock on my door.

Treat

My favorite treat of all time is chicken. Once I smell it, I'll turn on my cute puppy dog eyes and stare at my owner until she gives in and gives me some. It always works, she's weak. I always get some yummy chicken.
